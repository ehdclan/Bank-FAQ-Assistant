WEMA BANK & ALAT GENERAL BANKING POLICIES

Wema Bank Plc, licensed by the Central Bank of Nigeria (CBN), operates ALAT as Africa’s first fully digital bank since 2017. All services are delivered via ALAT app, USSD (*945#), web, and branches. Policies comply with CBN guidelines, NDPR 2019, AML/CFT laws, and international standards (ISO 27001, PCI-DSS). Customers must provide BVN, valid ID, and consent to auto-debits/terms.

KEY PRINCIPLES
• Compliance: Strict adherence to CBN, SEC, NDPA, AML/CFT, and global sanctions.
• Account Currency: Naira or USD (domiciliary); payable only in legal tender.
• Fees: Transaction charges, maintenance fees, overdraft interest at bank’s discretion.
• Security: Multi-factor authentication (PIN, OTP, hardware token); customer liable if credentials shared.
• Dispute Resolution: Report via 0700-078-7753 or purpleconnect@wemabank.com; 24/7 fraud monitoring.
• Termination: Bank may freeze/close accounts for fraud, inactivity, or regulatory breach.
• Governing Law: Laws of the Federal Republic of Nigeria; disputes resolved in Lagos courts.

1. ACCOUNT OPENING & MANAGEMENT
   • Eligibility:
        – Age 18+ (13–17 with guardian for ALAT Xplore; 0–12 for Royal Kiddies).
        – Valid NIN/BVN, phone number, email.
   • Account Types: Savings, Current, Domiciliary (USD), ALAT Xplore, Royal Kiddies.
   • Opening Process: Fully digital; zero minimum balance for basic ALAT account.
   • Customer Obligations:
        – Bound by all bank rules.
        – Notify bank immediately of any change in details.
        – No illegal or fraudulent use.
   • Bank Rights:
        – Honor withdrawals only if funds available.
        – Charge interest on overdrafts at prevailing rate.
        – Set-off debts across any accounts without notice.
   • Closure: 30 days’ notice required; bank may close instantly for fraud/inactivity.
   • Key Clause: “Any mail sent to last known address shall be considered duly delivered.”

2. ELECTRONIC & DIGITAL BANKING TERMS (ALAT App, *945#, Internet Banking)
   • Services: Balance inquiry, intra/inter-bank transfers, bill payments, loan applications, card control, etc.
   • Access Requirements: Username, password, transaction PIN, OTP (SMS/email/token), security questions.
   • Availability: 24/7 on best-effort basis; no liability for downtime or technical glitches.
   • Customer Responsibilities:
        – Safeguard all credentials.
        – Log out after use; change compromised details immediately.
        – Report unauthorized transactions instantly.
        – Liable for losses if credentials shared.
   • Bank Responsibilities:
        – Authenticate via security details.

   • Key Clause: “Issuance of any instruction with Account Security Details is sufficient confirmation.”

   • Data Controller: Wema Bank Plc.

        – Digital: IP, device info, cookies, transaction logs.

4. FRAUD MANAGEMENT POLICY
   • Objective: Prevent, detect, respond; 95% fraud loss reduction in 2024.
   • Coverage: All channels (branches, ALAT app, cards, USSD, web).
   • Prevention:
        – 24/7 AI monitoring.
        – OTP & multi-factor authentication.
        – Phishing awareness campaigns.
   • Detection & Response:
        – Automated alerts; instant account freeze if suspicious.
        – Dedicated Fraud Protection Teams.
        – Recovery via set-off or legal action.
   • Customer Duty:
        – Never share PIN/OTP.
        – Report fraud via separate device/channel.
   • Bank Liability: None if customer negligence proven.
